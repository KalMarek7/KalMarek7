About me

I began my IT journey in 2019 when I joined Atos GDC Poland, providing L1 support as a Service Desk Agent. After one year on the desk, I was promoted to streamline knowledge management, agent training initiatives, and all other activities related to service desk operations support.


My transition into engineering started as a result of a major automation push. I was appointed to lead a specialized team tasked with the end-to-end management of an AI-powered chatbot. In this role, I was responsible for the design and implementation of complex user flows, establishing myself as the Subject Matter Expert (SME) for chatbot operations within the organization. My success in optimizing these processes led me to consult with other teams, helping them replicate these efficiencies for their respective customers.


As I collaborated more closely with technical support teams to enhance chatbot capabilities by integrating ServiceNow and Nexthink APIs, I transitioned into a dedicated technical role. Since then, my focus has been to become a skilled programmer, starting with Python for back-end logic and automation, and utilizing Node.js and vanilla JavaScript for front-end integrations.
